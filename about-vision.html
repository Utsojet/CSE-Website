About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Barishal was established to equip students with advanced technological knowledge and skills necessary to compete globally. Our faculty members are highly dedicated, providing quality education and nurturing the next generation of innovators. Currently, the department offers B.Sc. (Engg.) and MS programs, and we aspire to start a PhD program in the near future. Our graduates contribute significantly to the nation's ICT development and maintain an excellent reputation both locally and internationally.

Vision

To become a world-renowned leader in Computer Science and Engineering education and research, shaping the future of technology and empowering individuals to develop innovative solutions addressing the world's most pressing challenges.

Mission

To advance the field of Computer Science and Engineering through excellence in education, research, and innovation, while nurturing a community of ethical, responsible, and lifelong learners who contribute meaningfully to society and the global ICT landscape.
